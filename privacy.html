Privacy Policy

TL;DR: We collect minimal data (your prediction and optional username). No tracking, no ads, no selling your data. Your IP is hashed for spam prevention only.

What We Collect

When you submit a prediction, we store:

  • Your prediction: Number of days until you think the AI bubble will pop
  • Username (optional): Only if you choose to provide one
  • Timestamp: When you submitted your prediction
  • Hashed IP address: For spam prevention and rate limiting (not stored in readable form)

Why We Collect It

  • Predictions: To calculate and display the community average
  • Username: To potentially show who predicted correctly in the future
  • IP hash: To prevent spam and abuse (rate limiting: 1 submission per 5 minutes)

Cookies

We don't set any cookies ourselves. However, Cloudflare (our hosting provider) may set strictly necessary cookies for:

  • DDoS protection
  • Bot detection
  • Load balancing

These cookies are essential for the site to function and don't require consent under GDPR.

Third Parties

We use:

  • Cloudflare: Hosting and DDoS protection
  • Cloudflare D1: Database storage

We do NOT use:

  • Google Analytics or any tracking
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media trackers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Not be tracked or profiled

Since we don't collect email addresses, contact us via GitHub if you need to exercise these rights.

Data Retention

We keep predictions indefinitely to maintain the historical record and show who predicted correctly. IP hashes are kept for rate limiting purposes only.

Security

We take security seriously:

  • IP addresses are hashed (SHA-256) before storage
  • All data is stored in Cloudflare's secure infrastructure
  • No passwords or sensitive data collected

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Check this page occasionally for changes.
